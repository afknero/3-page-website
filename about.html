Why This Project?

Voting is one of the most important ways we can shape our future, but many young people feel disconnected. Through this project, I want to dive into the reasons why Gen Z isn't showing up at the polls.

Gen Z and Politics

Despite being the most connected generation, Gen Z faces unique challenges in political engagement. We'll explore the disconnect between civic education, social media, and actual voting behavior.

Proposed Solutions

It's time to rethink how we talk about voting. From down-ballot races to local issues, I'll offer solutions that might inspire Gen Z to see voting as a tool for change rather than a chore.
