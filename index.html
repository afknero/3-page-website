Low Turnout, High Stakes

As Gen Z emerges as a major voting bloc, participation remains worryingly low. With critical issues at stake, understanding why this generation feels alienated is key to addressing the problem.

What Holds Us Back?

From feeling uninformed to mistrust in the political system, young voters cite numerous reasons for staying away from the polls. Identifying these barriers is the first step in creating solutions.

Voting isn't just about the presidential election. Local races often affect our lives the most. Understanding down-ballot candidates and issues can increase engagement and empower voters.
